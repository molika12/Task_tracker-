About Us

Welcome to Task Tracker, your personal productivity companion. Our mission is to help you stay consistent, accountable, and motivated every day. With features like daily task tracking, streak visualization, and a leaderboard for friendly competition, our platform turns your goals into achievable habits. You’ll also receive smart email reminders at three key moments throughout the day — even when you're offline — to keep your momentum going. Whether you're working on personal growth or professional goals, Task Tracker is here to keep you on track.

How To Use

1. Add Your Task
Add Your Task After logging in, click “+ Add Task” to enter your task title along with start and end dates. Each task will be tracked day by day.

2. Mark Completion Daily
Open your task and mark each day as “present” after completing the task.
This helps build your streak and
stay consistent.

3. View Progress & Leaderboard
Track your performance through detailed progress charts and compare
your streak with others
on the leaderboard.
